The Way We Work

From first sketch to final burnish.

A deliberate, hands-on process built on patience, provenance, and precision.

01

Selection

We source only the finest sustainably harvested hardwoods. Each slab is hand-selected for its unique character, ensuring the grain pattern complements the final form of the piece.

02

Shaping

No CNC machines. No shortcuts. Every joint is cut by hand using traditional chisels and saws. This ensures a level of precision and longevity that modern manufacturing simply cannot replicate.

03

Refinement

We finish our works with organic oils and waxes, applied in multiple coats and buffed to a soft, tactile sheen. It protects the wood while allowing you to feel the living material beneath.
