BUILT TOGETHER

At Tandem, we build lasting partnerships based on shared vision and sustainable growth. We're experts at development, construction and property management, with a track record of delivering successful real estate projects.

The long game

We're long term thinkers. Timing the market is a coin flip, but patience is our edge. By leveraging HUD-backed, fixed-rate, non-recourse financing, we create lasting stability and reliable yield.

Deliberate Growth

We advance one to two projects at a time. For us, success is about doing things right – bringing steadiness and clarity to everything we do.

Off the Beaten Path

We focus on neighborhoods poised for growth — close to the energy of major hubs, yet with room to grow. By leveraging Opportunity Zone incentives, we align with long-term value creation.

Skin in the Game

We believe the best partnerships are built on shared commitment. That’s why we invest alongside our partners. We’re all working toward the same goals.

Avoid the Herd

When markets get overheated, we get cautious. When fear takes over, we lean in. Opportunity and value is rarely where everyone else is looking.

ALL-IN OWNERSHIP

TM

We practice All-in Ownership™. That means we take full accountability for every project we touch — no shortcuts, no excuses, just total commitment from start to finish.

At Tandem ownership is a mindset. We plan like developers, design like managers and act like long-term stakeholders. That means we anticipate problems, sweat the details early and align teams quickly – all, to protect value and avoid surprises.

Because we manage development, construction, and property operations under one roof, we’re able to move faster, communicate better, and stay focused on what matters most. We don't pass the buck — we stay in it. From the first sketch to the final walk-through (and beyond), All-In Ownership™ is what ensures our projects are smarter, smoother, and built to last.

HOW WE GOT HERE

1997

The Starting Line

Tandem Construction, Inc. is founded by Tony Andrews as a carpentry contracting company.

Tony Andrews

2003

An Airport Conversation

Old friends, Tony Andrews and Dimitri Nassis, reconnect at O’Hare Airport, sparking the idea of pursuing real estate opportunities together.

O’Hare airport, Chicago

2004

First Steps, First Office

Dimitri Nassis joins Tandem full-time after leaving a career in options trading at the CBOE. That year, Tandem begins building single-family homes on Chicago’s North Side and establishes its first office at 1720 W School St in Roscoe Village.

1720 W School St – Tandem’s First Office

2006

Breaking Ground

Tandem begins construction on Burling Place, its first multi-unit condominium project at 1725 N Burling St in Lincoln Park.

Dimitri & Tony at Burling Place during construction

2008-13

Resilience in a Recession

Tandem navigates the Great Financial Crisis by executing third-party construction work, including the historic renovation of the Conway Mansion at Sacred Heart School and the expansion of the Union League Boys & Girls Club #1 in Pilsen.

Union League Boys & Girls Club

2013-15

Jumping Back In

Tandem returns to single-family home development as markets recover, highlighted by the renovation and addition at 344 South in Glencoe and the Fulton Car Gallery.

Fulton Car Gallery

2016-17

Going Multifamily

Tandem embarks on its first multifamily development, MODE Logan Square at 2501 W Armitage, in partnership with Chris Fifield of Fifield Cos., and completes construction and stabilization the following year.

MODE completed in 2017

2018-20

A Legacy Project Rises

Tandem develops Avenir in River West, a 196-unit, 23-story tower and the firm’s first high-rise project, undertaken as a legacy development.

Avenir in River West, Chicago

2020-21

Pandemic. Progress.

Tandem begins construction on Avra West Loop in April 2020, delivering the 20-story, 198-unit project in the middle of the COVID-19 pandemic, successfully navigating zoning, financing, and construction complications amid supply chain disruptions.

Avra West Loop completed in 2021

2022

A Full Circle Moment

Tandem completes its first “round-trip” transaction with the sale of MODE Logan Square.

2022-23

New Heights, New Milestones

Tandem begins construction on Sage West Loop in May 2022, completing the 18-story, 198-unit project in 11 months. Sage also marks the firm’s first use of a HUD 221(d)(4) loan.

Sage West Loop, Chicago

2024

Tampa, Here We Come

Tandem expands beyond Chicago with 2500 N Tampa, a Qualified Opportunity Zone multifamily and retail development in Tampa Heights, Florida.

Rendering of 2550 N Tampa St.
